About Asthma

Common Symptoms

Wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness, and persistent cough, especially at night or early morning.

Common Triggers

Allergens, exercise, cold air, stress, smoke, strong odors, and respiratory infections.

When to See a Doctor

Frequent symptoms, difficulty sleeping due to breathing, or if quick-relief medications aren't helping.
